• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozzarella
    • Pepper Jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  3. Add 4 slices of cheese on top of the bread.
  4. Cover with the second slice of bread, butter side up.
  5. Cook until golden brown on each side.
